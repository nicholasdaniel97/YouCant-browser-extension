YouCan’t is a browser extension design to edit the usual experience performed while using YouTube. During a typical experience, the user is exposed to many different inputs and a continuous loading of contents with the aim of presenting an infinite choice; many times, though, the results is a high sense of disorientation. Through a series of blocking and blurring, the user’s free will is removed within the platform: as a result, the choice of the video(s) to watch will be imposed exclusively by the YouTube algorithm. The user will then face two possibilities: to adapt and go through the interface’s options or ending the experience.
